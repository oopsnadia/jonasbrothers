THE BROTHERS

Nick Jonas

Nicholas Jerry Jonas was born on September 16, 1992 in Dallas, Texas. Singer, songwriter and actor, Nick is the youngest brother of the group and the one who formed the band.

Joe Jonas

Joseph Adam Jonas was born on August 15, 1989 in Casa Grande, Arizona. Singer, songwriter, and actor, Joe is the middle brother and frontman of the band.

Kevin Jonas

Paul Kevin Jonas II was born on November 5, 1987 in Teaneck, New Jersey. Guitarist and vocalist, Kevin is the oldest of his brothers.
